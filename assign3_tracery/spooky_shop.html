Morgan's Sojourn

Instructions

Players: 2 or more

Introduction: Every night at midnight you have been plagued by visions of an odd shop and the thing that runs it. One such vision traps you unless you play its game and solve its puzzle.

Goal: Figure out what time the shop's owner will return before they return. They will return after a fixed number of guesses.

Set-Up: Click the 'And then...' button below to generate a shop description.

Play: Take turns guessing what time the shop's owner will return by entering a time in "The time is now:" and clicking "The time shifts..."

Note: Whether you enter your guess in 24hr or 12hr time, is based on your personal settings. Regardless, your guess will be automatically converted to 24hr time and printed out in the "The time was:" section.

You can use items to help you. Items will either tell you if the hour (0-23) or the minute (0-59) of your guess is equal to, greater than, or less than the correct time. However, each item only works within a certain time range. Use the descriptions of what changes after the clock ticks to figure out the ranges. Pay attention to the feedback below your guess to determine if you've used the item in the correct range.

You gain items by completing challenges. A challenge is generated every time you make a guess. If you successfully complete or win the challenge, you get the item generated. You can use that item once before it is exhausted. The item is not considered used if it returns to your hands. Every player should keep track of the items they have.

You can use the item that the shop sells as many times as you want. For example if the shop that is generated is “an oil lamp shop”, you can use “oil lamp” as many times as you like.

Note: Items are case and space sensitive. Be sure to mind spaces and use the singular for shop items.
